About Us

Join us and discover why Altius Gym is the trusted choice for fitness enthusiasts across Lahore.

Services

At Altius Gym, we offer a comprehensive range of fitness services designed to meet all your health and wellness goals. Cardiovascular Equipment

Our cardio zone features state-of-the-art equipment including treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, rowing machines, and stair climbers. Each machine is equipped with heart rate monitoring, customizable workout programs, and entertainment systems to keep you motivated. Whether you're training for endurance, burning calories, or improving heart health, our diverse range of cardio equipment provides effective workouts for all fitness levels in a climate-controlled environment.

Strength Training Equipment

Build muscle and increase strength with our comprehensive selection of strength training equipment. Our facility features Olympic weightlifting platforms, power racks, smith machines, and a complete range of free weights including dumbbells, barbells, and weight plates. We also offer specialized machines for every major muscle group - chest presses, leg presses, lat pulldowns, shoulder presses, and cable crossover stations. All equipment is commercial-grade, regularly maintained, and organized to provide an efficient and safe training environment for strength athletes of all levels.

Group Fitness Classes

Join our energetic group fitness classes designed to motivate and challenge you in a supportive community environment. We offer diverse class formats including high-intensity interval training (HIIT), yoga, Pilates, cycling, Zumba, strength conditioning, and boot camp sessions. Led by certified instructors who provide modifications for all fitness levels, our classes combine motivating music, expert guidance, and group energy to make workouts enjoyable and effective. Whether you're looking to burn calories, build strength, improve flexibility, or relieve stress, there's a class perfect for your goals and schedule.

Other Services

Beyond our core fitness offerings, we provide comprehensive wellness services to support your complete health journey. Our additional services include personalized one-on-one training sessions, nutritional counseling and meal planning, body composition analysis, fitness assessments, and recovery services including stretching areas and foam rolling stations. We also offer locker rooms with showers, towel service, complimentary Wi-Fi, and dedicated functional training zones. Whether you need individual attention, recovery support, or advanced performance tracking, our additional services ensure you have everything needed for success.
